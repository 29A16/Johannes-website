Deserts

Milchreis (rice Porridge)

  • 200 g short grain rice
  • 200 ml water
  • 500 ml milk
  • vanilla sugar to taste
  • cinnamon to taste

After washing the rice, add water and bring to a boil. Then lower to medium heat and add the milk and let simmer for 30 min or until the rice is tender. Add seasoning to taste.

Coconut variant

  • 200 g rice
  • 300 ml water

Cook the rice, while preparing the sauce

  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Mango/fruit topping/Sesame seeds

Mix sauce and rice, add topping

Basic pancakes

  • 500 g flour
  • 4 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Milk to desired consistency (~600 g)

Mix flour, sugar, and salt with milk. Add the baking powder just before frying the batter.

Poppy seed cake

  • 500 g flour
  • 200 g sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 250 g butter
  • 1 egg

Mix together slowly (leave the butter chilled) until crumbly. Use half of the dough as base and crumble the rest on top later after adding the filling.

  • 1 L milk
  • 150 g sugar
  • 250 g poppy seeds (original recipe 350 g)
  • 160 g semolina
  • 100 g butter

Solve the sugar in milk and bring to a boil, add the semolina and let cook for a view minutes before adding the poppy seeds under stirring. When the mass thickens remove from stove and add the butter. Let rest a view minutes before adding to the dough base. Bake at 180 degree C for 40 min. Let cool before removing from the baking form.

Chocolate cheesecake

(original 40% less)

  • 266 g Oreos
  • 63 g melted butter

Crush the Oreos into fine powder and add the butter. Mix together and form the base of the Cake → Freezer until solid

  • 322 g choklad
  • 560 g cream cheese
  • 168 g icing sugar
  • 1.4 tbsp of vanilla
  • 1.4 pinches if sea salt

Homogenize creamed cheese and add molten chocolate, mix. Add whisked sugar and flavors. 

  • 175 g whipping cream

Whip the cream and gently fold it under the batter. Add the batter on top of the base and put into the fridge over night/until soil.

  • 119 g chocolate
  • 154 g whipping cream

Heat whipping cream until simmering and add to the chocolate, let rest for 3 min. Mix and add on top of the batter. Into the fridge for 1 h → add toppings (raspberries).
