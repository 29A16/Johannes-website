Main courses

Pizza and Pasta

Penne Arrabiata

  • 300 g penne
  • 500 ml sieved tomato’s
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4-5 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 tsp salt
  • opt fresh Basil
  • opt 2 tbsp tomato purée

While cooking the penne in salted water finely chop/crush the garlic and fry together with the chili flakes under medium heat until the garlic just starts to turn brown. To the garlic add ca. 50 ml of penne water and 500 ml of sieved tomatoes. Combine sieved pasta with sauce. Optionally add fresh Basil

Pizza

  • 400g flower
  • 225 ml warm water
  • 1 pack fresh yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp olive oil

Mix everything and let rest until doubled in size. Divide into separate loafs and let rest for 20 min before stretching into pizza base and adding toppings.

Soup

Pea soup

  • 700 g frozen peas
  • olive oil
  • 3 small onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 pack of vegetable stock
  • optional heavy cream, ham/sausage, croutons

Roast roughly chopped onions and garlic before adding peas and vegetable stock. Let simmer until the peas are tender. Season with pepper and salt to taste. Puree and add heavy cream/croutons.
